Gloucester County Educational Campus

The Gloucester County Educational Campus is located on 298 acres in Deptford, New Jersey. Home to Rowan College of South Jersey- Gloucester Main Campus, Gloucester County Institute of Technology and Gloucester County Special Services School District, the campus provides educational opportunities, innovative and best practice programs in state-of-the-art facilities.
